## Character Quick Reference

**ILAN** (Will Graham archetype)
- Mortal investigator for the Tribunal with a shard of the dead god's mind embedded in his skull
- Can touch blood/bone and reconstruct crimes through the killer's perspective
- Public mask: Competent, steady, quietly brilliant
- True self: Fractured, empathic to the point of dissolution, terrified of his own darkness
- **Core lie**: "If I understand monsters too well, I must be one."
- Trauma: The Night of Shattered Vows gave him the god-shard; it speaks to him

**DR. MARROW** (Hannibal archetype)
- Ancient bone-wight physician-priest; elegant, cultured, aesthetic about everything
- Heals the city's monsters during the day; ritually kills "the unworthy" at night
- Public mask: Gentle healer, trusted advisor, sophisticated host
- True self: Predator with genuine appreciation for beauty, including Ilan's unique mind
- **Core desire**: Not just to possess Ilan, but to cultivate him‚Äîsee what he could become
- **Sees love as**: Transformation; making someone more fully themselves (even if monstrous)

## Prime Contract Thread (Book 3)

- **Local villain**: Dr. Marrow himself (antagonist-love interest) + a god-remnant avatar
- **Connection to Big Bad**: Marrow was designed by the Prime Contract as proof that monsters can be "redeemed" into saints‚Äîwhile secretly culling threats to the system
- **What Ilan & Marrow accomplish**: Prove the Prime Contract has infected justice and medicine; kill a physical avatar; become wildcards in exile
