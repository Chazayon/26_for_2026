## Character Quick Reference

**SOLEN** (Stanford Pines archetype)
- Senior Archivist of the Halo Ward's Restricted Stacks‚Äîbrilliant, isolated, haunted
- Six fingers on one hand (born mutation, marks him as "touched by the god")
- Has spent decades mapping contracts, Redemption Marks, and institutional corruption
- Public mask: Competent, scholarly, slightly eccentric researcher
- True self: Consumed by guilt over "the translation error" that let Cipher in decades ago
- **Core lie**: "If I can understand the system completely, I can fix what I broke."

**CIPHER** (Bill Cipher archetype)
- Eldritch thought-form: the personality of the Prime Contract's original signature
- Trapped in the dead god's pupil/dreamscape; appears as jaunty, reality-warping demon
- Public mask: Loud, cartoonish, showman god‚Äîjokes, puns, chaotic energy
- True self: Genuinely intelligent, genuinely bored, genuinely obsessed with Solen
- **Core desire**: End the Prime Contract‚Äînot from mercy, but for total freedom from all rules
- **Sees love as**: Possession, fascination, endless obsession with someone who can match him

## Prime Contract: The Final Confrontation

- **The Big Bad**: The Prime Contract / The Nameless Creditor‚Äîthe memory-eating entity that all four books have been chipping away at
