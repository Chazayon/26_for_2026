## Character Quick Reference

**CYRAN** (Astarion archetype)
- Vampiric "Blood Relic" forged from the dead god's blood
- Former temple ornament, used for centuries in sacred orgies and executions
- Public mask: Charming, theatrical, flirtatious predator

- **Core lie**: "I am only as valuable as the pleasure and violence I can provide."

**LYSA** (Tav archetype)  
- Scribe-investigator for the Redemption Tribunal

- Public mask: Professional, pragmatic, incisive
- True self: Deeply compassionate with a hard spine, fears becoming numb to suffering
- **Core belief**: "Redemption is possible, but not automatic‚Äîand not owed."

- **What Cyran & Lysa accomplish**: Damage the Prime Contract's heart-clause, creating a weakness Solen will exploit in Book 4
