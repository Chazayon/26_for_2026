#### **Plot & Structure**

**Plot Summary:**  
Lysa shouts from the entrance, drawing all eyes. She doesn't attack physically yet; she attacks legally/magically. She shouts: *"I Audit this Ceremony!"* (Absurd, but effective distraction). Thessen commands Cyran to finish it, but Lysa shouts a Counter-Command using the loophole she figured out: The Command Key binds him to "Obedience," but doesn't specify *to whom*.
