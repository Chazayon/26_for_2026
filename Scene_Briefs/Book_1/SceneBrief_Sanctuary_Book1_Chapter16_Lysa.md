They analyze the Gala intel: Thessen isn't just selling "shares" of Cyran; he's selling *control keys*. Temple magic that can compel obedience. #### **Action Beats (25 Minimum)**

1. Lysa drawing lines on the floor-boards. "Thessen connects to the Tribunal here."
2. Cyran enters. Hair damp, wearing borrowed roughspun shirt.
3. He hands her a mug. "It's sludge. Drink up."
4. He sits on the floor opposite her. Knees touching.
5. "So, we're fugitives," he says lightly.
6. "We're independent investigators," she corrects.
7. She points to the map. "The Gala. He mentioned 'Control Keys'."
8. Cyran stiffens. "Temple magic. Command words."
9. "Do you have one?" she asks gently.
10. "I don't know. I have... gaps." He taps his temple.
11. "If he has the key, he doesn't need to persuade you. He can just drive you."
12. Lysa shudders. "Then we need the user manual."
13. "The Hollow Archive," Cyran says. "In the Skull."
14. "It's forbidden," she notes.
15. "It's where they made me. The records will be there."
16. She looks at him. "Going back there... are you sure?"
17. "No. But I'm sure I don't want to be a puppet."
18. She circles the 'Skull District' on the floor.
19. "We go tonight."
20. He reaches out. Traces the charcoal line.
21. "You're risking your career for a relic."
22. "I'm risking it for a partner," she says.
23. He smiles. A real one. Small, shy.
24. "Partner. I like that."
25. They finish the coffee.
26. He helps her clean the floor (destroying evidence).
27. The plan is set.

*Note: The shift to "Partner" is the key beat.*
