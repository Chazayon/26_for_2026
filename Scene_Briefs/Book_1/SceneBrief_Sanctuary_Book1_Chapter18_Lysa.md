#### **Plot & Structure**

**Plot Summary:**  
They infiltrate the Skull District—the seat of the old Religion. They enter the Hollow Archive, a library where books are bound in... questionable materials. Cyran is on edge, memories surfacing. They navigate silent guardians (bone-constructs) using Cyran's old Temple pass-phrases (which he barely remembers). They find the "Registry of Vessels." Lysa reads Cyran's entry. It details his "creation"—he was a street urchin modified into a "Relic" via alchemy and magic. It lists his "Command Key"—a phrase that strips his will. *Key: "Obedience is the only Virtue."* Lysa memorizes it (to protect him) but vows never to use it. They destroy the record, but Cyran reveals Thessen already has a copy—that's what the Gala was about.

**Scene Goal:**  
Lysa: Find the truth about the Keys.  
Cyran: Face his origin.

**Scene Outcome:**  
They confirm the existence of the Key. They destroy the original record.

**Scene Question:**  
What are the words that enslave him?

**Ticking Clock/Deadline:**  
Guardians are waking up.

**Opening Hook - First Line Strategy:**

1. *The Skull District stares at you, literally. The windows are carved like sockets.*
2. *Books should smell like paper and glue, not dried meat and regret.*
3. *Cyran steps into the archive like a ghost haunting his own grave.*
